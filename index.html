Instructions The goal is to be the first player to reach the winning score or above.
Winning score is 100 by default. You can change this and hit "New Game" to update the winning score.
2 dice will be rolled on every roll. The combined total will be added to the players current score. Once the player chooses to Hold, the current score will be added to the total score and it is the next players turn.
If one of the dice rolls a 1, the player loses his current score and it is the next players turn.
If the left die rolls a 6 two turns in a row, the player will lose his current AND total score, and it is the next players turn.
